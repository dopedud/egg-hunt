> the egg to find a key, you discover something belonging to the missing kids, items or body parts. You are a kid
> partaking in a Saw like death game to find the way out. Or the price is part of you will be the price in the eggs.

> You stumble across a request to collect eggs in a certain forest. The request rewards you generously if you did your
